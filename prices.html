Prices

Tree Removal

Tree removal prices can vary widely based on several factors, including the size, species, condition, and location of the tree, as well as local market rates and the complexity of the job. On average, you can expect to pay anywhere from $150 to $2,000 or more for tree removal services. For smaller trees, such as those under 30 feet tall, you might pay between $150 to $700. Medium-sized trees, ranging from 30 to 60 feet, typically cost between $300 to $1,200 to remove. Larger trees, over 60 feet tall, can cost anywhere from $800 to $2,000 or more to remove.



Tree Maintenance

Tree maintenance prices can vary widely depending on several factors such as the size of the tree, its location, the services required, and the expertise of the professionals involved. Generally, basic tree maintenance services like trimming, pruning, or shaping can cost anywhere from $100 to $1000 per tree, with smaller trees on the lower end of the scale and larger or more complex trees on the higher end.



Tree Trimming

Tree trimming prices can vary widely depending on several factors including the size of the tree, its location, the extent of trimming required, and the company you hire. On average, expect to pay between $250 to $1,500 for tree trimming services. Small trees under 30 feet tall typically cost between $80 to $300 to trim, while medium-sized trees between 30 to 60 feet tall might range from $300 to $700. Larger trees over 60 feet tall can cost anywhere from $700 to $1,500 or more. Additional factors that can affect the cost include the tree's condition, accessibility, and any extra services like debris removal or stump grinding. Emergency services or trimming trees near power lines might also incur higher costs due to the added risk and specialized equipment needed.



Stump Removal

Stump removal prices vary depending on factors like stump size, location, accessibility, and the method of removal. On average, stump removal can cost anywhere from $60 to $350 per stump. Smaller stumps with diameters less than 12 inches might fall on the lower end of the price spectrum, while larger stumps or those in hard-to-reach areas could cost more.



Stump Grinding

Stump grinding prices vary based on factors like stump size, location, accessibility, and any additional services required. On average, you can expect to pay between $75 to $300 for a single stump, with larger stumps costing more to remove. Small stumps (less than 12 inches in diameter) typically cost less to grind, ranging from $75 to $150. Medium-sized stumps (12 to 24 inches in diameter) may cost between $150 to $300. Large stumps (greater than 24 inches) can cost upwards of $300 or more.
